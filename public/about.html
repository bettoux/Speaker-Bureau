Our Vision

Facettes transcends the simple representation of speakers; it is committed to offering conversations that go beyond conventional thinking. We aspire to stimulate thought and open doors to new ideas and horizons, thus transforming each event into a captivating intellectual experience.

We are proud to represent experts with exceptional backgrounds, brimming with novel and innovative approaches. Their talks do not simply reflect their expertise; they shape new perspectives and inspire concrete changes.

At Facettes, we are the ambassadors for "change makers" who, too busy making an impact in their environment, deserve to have their vision and ideas shared. Our mission goes beyond traditional presentations; it is a commitment to amplifying influential voices that are shaping the world around them.

In summary, Facettes' mission is to be the catalyst for dialogues and thoughts that transcend boundaries, thereby carrying the message of visionaries busy making an impact on the world. This is our commitment; this is our mission.

Our Values

We combine Curiosity for diverse narratives, Benevolence for robust support, and unwavering Integrity in all our partnerships.

Benevolence

Because it embodies our deep commitment to the well-being, growth, and success of our talent. We firmly believe that for our representatives to shine fully, they need constant moral support, attentive listening, and a kind approach. Benevolence allows us to establish authentic relationships with our talent, to understand their needs and challenges, and to support them positively at every stage of their journey. By fostering an environment where benevolence prevails, we help our talent develop their self-confidence, overcome obstacles, and realize their potential.

Integrity

Because it is at the heart of our commitment to our talent, clients, and partners. It represents our pledge to act honestly, transparently, and ethically in all our interactions. Integrity guides us in the fair management of contracts, in respecting the rights of our talent, and in preserving their reputation and image. By placing integrity at the forefront of our actions, we establish a solid foundation of trust and mutual respect, which strengthens the credibility of our agency and the satisfaction of our talent and clients.

Curiosity

Because it embodies our deep commitment to continuous learning, open-mindedness, and the discovery of the many facets of cultural diversity in Canada. We firmly believe that for our representatives to shine fully, it is essential to explore new horizons, understand multiple experiences, and grasp diverse life stories. Curiosity drives us to ask questions, challenge stereotypes, learn constantly, and expand our horizons, thus contributing to authentic and inclusive representation.
